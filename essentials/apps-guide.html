Essential Apps

Must-download apps for traveling in China

Home/Travel Essentials/Apps

Download Before Arrival

All these apps are available on iOS App Store and Google Play. Download them before arriving in China since Google Play is not accessible from within China.

Payment Apps

Alipay

The most essential app for China. Use it for everything from street food to metro rides. Supports international credit cards.

WeChat

More than just messaging—WeChat Pay, mini-programs, and integrated services. Essential for communicating with locals.

Transportation

DiDi (滴滴出行)

China's dominant ride-hailing app, similar to Uber. English interface available. Must have a Chinese phone number or WeChat for verification.

Amap (高德地图)

The best navigation app for China, with English support. Use for driving, walking, or public transit directions. Works offline.

Metro Man

Shows metro routes in major Chinese cities with English. Essential for navigating subway systems in Beijing, Shanghai, etc.

Translation

Apple Translate

Built into iOS, works offline for Chinese-English translation. Simple and effective.

Baidu Translate

Excellent translation app with photo translation (point camera at Chinese text), conversation mode, and voice translation.

Travel & Booking

Trip.com

All-in-one booking app for flights, hotels, trains, and cars. English interface, accepts international cards.

12306

Official app for booking China Railway high-speed train tickets. English version available.

Communication

The "app for everything" in China. Messaging, voice calls, video calls, payments, mini-programs, and more. Download before arrival.

VPN App

Don't forget your VPN! We recommend ExpressVPN, NordVPN, or similar for accessing blocked services.
