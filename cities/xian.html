Why Visit Xi'an

Xi'an is one of China's ancient capitals and the starting point of the Silk Road. It's home to one of the most significant archaeological discoveries in history—the Terracotta Army—and retains much of its medieval city walls and atmosphere. The city offers a compelling journey through 3,000 years of Chinese civilization.

Top Attractions

🎭

Terracotta Army

⏰ 8:30-18:00🎫 ¥120

The mausoleum of China's first emperor, with over 8,000 life-sized clay soldiers. One of the most extraordinary archaeological sites in the world.

🏰

Xi'an City Wall

⏰ 8:00-22:00🎫 ¥54

Cycle or walk along the best-preserved ancient city wall in China. The 14km circuit offers excellent views of the old and new city.

🕌

Muslim Quarter

⏰ 24 hoursFree

A vibrant food street with hundreds of vendors selling lamb burgers, roujamo, and local snacks. Come hungry.
