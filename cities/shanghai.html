Why Visit Shanghai

Shanghai is China's most international city—a dazzling metropolis where Art Deco towers stand alongside ancient temples, and the futuristic Pudong skyline rivals anywhere in the world. It's the perfect introduction to modern China while retaining its unique colonial heritage along the Bund.

The city is exceptionally foreigner-friendly with extensive English signage, international dining options, and world-class infrastructure. Whether you want to explore historic Yu Garden, shop on Nanjing Road, or enjoy a cocktail atop the world's highest bars, Shanghai delivers.

Top Attractions

🌆

The Bund

⏰ 24 hoursFree

The iconic waterfront promenade with 52 buildings of different architectural styles. Best viewed at night when the Pudong lights illuminate.

🗼

Oriental Pearl Tower

⏰ 8:00-22:00¥180

Shanghai's most recognizable landmark with observation decks offering panoramic city views. The sphere at 268m is unforgettable.

🏛️

Yu Garden

⏰ 8:30-17:30¥40

A classical Chinese garden in the heart of the old city, featuring pavilions, bridges, and lotus pools from the Ming Dynasty.

🛍️

Nanjing Road

⏰ 24 hoursFree

The world's busiest shopping street with over 600 retailers. From luxury brands to local shops, there's something for everyone.
